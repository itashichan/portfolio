This interface prototype was created as an application design task for Buzzd8.
The brief was to create one of three types of pages for a "confessions" app and to take design inspiration from the video game Cult of the Lamb. I chose the thread view because it gave more opportunity to include a range of features, such as the reaction emoji faces, which are modelled on the cartoony CotL characters. The colour scheme and scribbly effect on many assets are also inspired by the game.

No feedback was received.


These two ads (for 1440 and Barestep) were created as part of a trial task for a job applied to at Adcrate. I was given an image to base each on, the website for the company it was for, and instructions to create it with Canva. In all honesty, I felt that the 1440 one didn't really reflect the branding on its website, but this is a close representation of the meme-style image given in the brief.

No feedback was received.
