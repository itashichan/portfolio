Typesetting Projects with Medibang Inc.

Since 2022, I've been lettering and redrawing sections of manga pages received remotely from Tokyo.
Aside from following strict rules regarding leading, tracking, layer styles, etc, there is also a need to proofread to correct typos or spot a missing translation. My basic Japanese allows me to save some time with this when the text in question is simple enough (2020年から日本語を独学で勉強しています!).
Three series I work on are currently being published by Shueisha through their Manga Plus app. Follow the links below to read them and past work for free.

    Current

  • Magilumiere Co. Ltd. by Sekka Iwata & Yu Aoki
  • MAD by Yusuke Otori
  • Metaphor: ReFantazio by ATLUS & Yoichi Amano

    Past
