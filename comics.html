"Elven treasure hunter Altin manages to kidnap the local prince completely by accident. Like, he knew he was breaking and entering, but never expected to be leaving with the (admittedly very attractive) Prince Zephyr. Or for the Prince to end up being so chill with it. Aside from the criminal nature of their meeting, things seem to go well, but then a slightly cliche regicide plot and attempt to usurp the throne derail their burgeoning relationship and leave both young men on the run."
